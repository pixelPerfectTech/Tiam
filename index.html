ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Welcome to TIAM - a Leading Environmental Service Company

Tiam Environmental Services,(TES) is a company that specializes in pest control and cleaning services. The company is licensed by the Environmental Health Officers Registration Council of Nigeria (EHORECON), Lagos State Environmental Protection Agency (LASEPA), Ogun State Ministry of Environment and other professional bodies.

The company has modern equipment that enable our chemicals to reach those obscure places that arbour those insects that cause ailments and diseases; even those that destroy stationeries and building materials added to the aforementioned. The company employs environmental friendly chemicals in accordance to the World Health Organization's (WHO) guidelines.
